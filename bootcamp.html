PYNQ Bootcamp

Discover the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) with the AMD KRIA-SOM KV260 Board!

This week-long summer course introduces students to the world of Artificial Intelligence focusing on actual deployment and real-world utility of the ever increasing power of Machine Learning and AI.The course is a natural evolution of PYNQ Bootcamp offered in previous years. The camp will include a wide range of topics, such as using AMD technology to enable computer vision, as well as gathering real-world data using various sensors and feeding it into AI models. By the conclusion of the course, you will be familiar with AMD platforms and appreciate real world application of AI.

  • This camp is geared towards students with some basic experience with either coding and/or electronics. Some Python experience is required.
